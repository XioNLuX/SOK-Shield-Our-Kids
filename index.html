Our Mission

Shield Our Kids (SOK) is dedicated to preventing school shootings and creating safer educational environments for all children. We believe that through early intervention, community awareness, and evidence-based prevention strategies, we can stop violence before it happens.

Our comprehensive approach addresses the root causes of school violence while building resilient communities that support every student's mental health and wellbeing. We work with schools, families, and communities to create environments where warning signs are recognized, help is accessible, and every child feels valued and supported.

Firearm Safety & Secure Storage

Proper firearm storage is one of the most effective ways to prevent school shootings, teen suicides, and accidental deaths. Every gun owner has a responsibility to secure their firearms from unauthorized access.

üîí Essential Storage Guidelines

ALWAYS follow these critical safety measures:

  • Double-lock system - safe + trigger lock minimum
  • Hide all keys and combinations - children are resourceful
  • Teach gun safety rules:
    • Stop - don't touch
    • Don't touch - leave it alone
    • Leave the area immediately
    • Tell an adult right away
  • Regular safety conversations - age-appropriate discussions
  • Monitor for depression/anger - extra vigilance during crises

üè† Home Security Best Practices

Comprehensive home firearm security:

  • Gun safes: Biometric, combination, or key-locked
  • Quick-access boxes: For home defense (adults only)
  • Ammunition storage: Separate locked container
  • Regular inventory: Know exactly what you have
  • Secure from visitors: Friends, relatives, workers
  • Travel security: Locked cases for transport
  • Insurance considerations: Coverage for theft/damage

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning Signs & Prevention

When to take extra precautions:

  • Mental health crises - temporary removal from home
  • Domestic violence situations - immediate secure storage
  • Substance abuse issues - enhanced security measures
  • Teen behavioral changes - depression, anger, isolation
  • Threats of self-harm - remove access completely
  • School disciplinary issues - increased monitoring

Remember: It's better to be overly cautious than to face tragedy.

Invest in quality security equipment:

  • Quick Access: GunVault, Fort Knox pistol boxes

  • Trigger Locks: Master Lock, Barska models
  • Ammunition Storage: MTM, Plano lockable boxes
